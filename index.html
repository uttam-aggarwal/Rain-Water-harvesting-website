Rain-Water Harvesting

Rainwater collecting is an important method of water conservation that can help ease water shortages and lessen the pressure on the environment caused by traditional water sources. With the increasing demand for sustainable solutions to address the water crisis, rainwater harvesting has become a popular and successful approach.

The website project was done with the purpose of creating a platform for the dissemination of information about rainwater harvesting to a large audience. The website sought to offer in-depth details about the advantages of rainwater harvesting, the many methods used, and helpful advice on how to put a rainwater harvesting system into place.

Why do we Harvest Rainwater?

The rainwater harvesting system is followed to support the conservation of water. Today, scarcity good quality water has become a significant cause of concern.However,rainwater,which is pure and of good quality, can be used for irrigation,washing,cooking.It reduces soil erosion and flood hazards by collecting rainwater and reducing the flow of stormwater to prevent urban flooding.
